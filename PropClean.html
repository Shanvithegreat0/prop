What Makes PropClean Different?

Manage and monitor your spaces effortlessly with PropClean. Real-time updates, efficient task tracking, and automated stock management—all in one powerful app.

  • Fully functional

    Fully integrated solution that streamlines workspace management, ensuring efficient task handling from start to finish.

  • Stock Management

    Never run out of essential supplies. PropClean tracks your inventory and alerts you when it’s time to reorder, ensuring you’re always prepared.

  • Secure Data

    All data is encrypted and secured, ensuring that only authorized users can access sensitive information. Your workspace’s privacy is our top priority.

  • Real-Time Dashboard

    Get a clear overview of all ongoing and completed tasks in one place, allowing for easy management and quick decision-making.

  • User-Friendly Interface

    Designed with simplicity in mind, the app is easy to navigate, ensuring that both staff and admins can use it effectively with minimal training.

  • Real-Time Monitoring

    Keep your spaces clean and compliant with photo-based updates. Supervisors can remotely monitor tasks and ensure standards are met without needing to be on-site.
